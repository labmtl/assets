This image is a screenshot of a website for an initiative called "Neuf." The top section features an aerial photograph of a neighborhood with houses, roads, and green spaces. Overlaid on this image is text in French that reads: "Neuf : Votre quartier prend vie ! Ensemble, on renouvelle notre quartier," which translates to "Neuf: Your neighborhood comes to life! Together, we renew our neighborhood."

Below the image, there is a navigation menu with options labeled "À propos," "Actions," "Engager," and "Contactez-nous." The main content area welcomes visitors to the Neuf site, describing it as a citizen initiative focused on cleanliness and renewal of the neighborhood. It emphasizes individual responsibility and collective action to improve the environment.

The "Notre mission" (Our mission) section explains that the Neuf initiative aims to promote a culture of cleanliness through individual actions and create a ripple effect for collective environmental stewardship.

At the bottom, there is a footer with copyright information for 2025, contact details (an email address), and a link to follow the initiative on Facebook.
